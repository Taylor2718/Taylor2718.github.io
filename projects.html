Satellite Detection Project

The Satellite Detection Project focuses on developing advanced algorithms for the detection and tracking of satellites in Earth's orbit. Leveraging principles of signal processing and machine learning, our goal is to enhance satellite monitoring capabilities for various applications, including space debris management and satellite communication optimization.
